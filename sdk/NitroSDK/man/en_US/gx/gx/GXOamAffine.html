GXOamAffine;

Elements

PA Affine transform parameter; amount of change in X direction on same line (dx)
PB Affine transform parameter; amount of change in X direction on next line (dmx)
PC Affine transform parameter; amount of change in Y direction on same line (dy)
PD Affine transform parameter; amount of change in Y direction on next line (dmy)

Description

This structure is for manipulating the OAM affine transform parameters, PA, PB, PC, and PD.
